Pork Schnitzel

Pork schnitzel is quick and easy dish made from ingredients most households have readily available and as a result is fairly low cost as well. It is an outstanding dish made even better by the dill sauce used over the pork.

ingredients

  • 4 boneless pork chops 1/2 inch thick (1 lb total)
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs or panko
  • 1 1/4 tsp paprike
  • 3 tbsp extra virigin olive oil
  • 3/4 cup chicken stock
  • 1/2 tsp dried dill
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Use meat hammer to pound pork cutlets to 1/4 - 1/8 inch thickness
  2. Cut small slits along edges of cutlets to prevent curling
  3. Setup breading station with 3 bowls:
    • 1 egg & milk in 1st bowl
    • Flour, salt, & pepper in a 2nd bowl
    • Combine breadcrumbs (or panko) and paprike in a 3rd bowl
  4. Dredge cutlets starting with flour then egg then breadcrumbs on all sides
  5. In a pan cook cutlets on each side in olive oil until golden on both sides
  6. Scrape all debris from the pan and deglaze with chicken stock
  7. Mix dill, salt, & sour cream together in a small bowl, stir the mixture into the chicken stock
  8. Heat and stir until mixture thickens (do not let boil)
  9. Serve cutlets with the sauce, lemon slices on the side (optional), & garnish with dill (optional)
